# 🚗 Sourcing Quality Interactive Dashboard – Retail Lending Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

Developed a comprehensive Power BI analytics and decision-support platform for a simulated retail auto-loan portfolio comprising **100,000+ customer accounts**. The solution integrates profitability analysis, sourcing quality assessment, credit risk monitoring, profitability forecasting, and What-If scenario simulations to evaluate the impact of customer acquisition and sourcing strategies on portfolio performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

Retail lending profitability depends heavily on customer credit quality, sourcing strategy, and portfolio composition. This project was designed to help answer critical business questions:

- How much profit has the portfolio generated?
- Which customer segments contribute the highest profit or loss?
- Is sourcing quality improving over time?
- What will future profitability look like if current trends continue?
- How will profitability change if sourcing strategies are modified?

---

## 🔍 Key Analytics Modules

### 1. Profitability Analysis

Analyze portfolio profitability across:

- CIBIL Score Bands
- Customer Profiles (Salaried / Self-Employed)
- New vs Upgrade Customers
- Sourcing Channels
- Vehicle Segments
- States & Manufacturers

**KPIs Covered**

- PBT%
- IRR%
- Revenue
- Recovery
- Profit per Loan
- Cost Components

### 2. Sourcing Quality & Risk Analysis

Evaluate credit performance through:

- Default Rate
- NPA Rate
- Bad Rate @ 12 MOB
- Bad Rate @ 24 MOB

This module helps identify high-risk customer segments and sourcing channels.

### 3. Future Profitability Forecasting

Built an ETS-based forecasting framework to:

- Analyze historical profitability trends
- Forecast future PBT performance
- Support portfolio planning and strategic decision-making

### 4. What-If Simulation Framework

Developed an advanced simulation engine enabling users to modify sourcing mix across:

- Channels
- CIBIL Bands
- Customer Profiles
- New/Upgrade Customers
- Vehicle Segments
- Pre-Approved vs Non-Pre-Approved Loans

The framework dynamically recalculates:

- PBT%
- IRR%
- Revenue
- Recovery
- Credit Cost
- Operating Costs

allowing decision-makers to evaluate the impact of alternative sourcing strategies before implementation.
